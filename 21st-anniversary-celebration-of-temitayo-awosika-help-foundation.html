August 12 â€” August 14, 2020

Temitayo Awosika Help Foundation (TAHF) was borne to immortalize the principles and way of life of Mr. Temitayo Awosika, born on the 12th of August, 1980 (40 years ago), and passed on 21 years ago on the 13th of August, 1999.

Indeed on the 12th of August, 2020 would have been the celebration of his 40th birthday. To celebrate this young man of Honour and Love, as well as celebrate the 21st anniversary of TAHF, the following activities have been put in place. TAHF is committed to upholding the life virtues of Temitayo held so dear, and to creating a global community linking resources and support for patients and families of sickle cell disease.
