Temitayo Awosika Help Foundation (TAHF) is a non-govermental, non profit making organisation.

It was set up to cater for children and adults living with sickle cell anaemia condition especially the under priviledged.

The vision of TAHF is to ensure healthy and productive life for sickle cell patients and the poor in the society through various programs and projects.

What we Did

TAHF Projects

Our well-articulated programmes span through provision of drugs and medicare services to patients in teaching and specialists hospitals across the country and research into herbal preparations for better management of sickle cell anaemia.

Health Care

TAHF MEDICATION SUPPLY PROGRAMME

Provision of routine drugs to persons with sickle cell conditions. These include paludine, Folice Acid, B. Complex, Vitamin C, Anti-Malaria, Anti-Biotics and Pain Reliever

Health Care

TAHF MEDICAL SERVICES

Providing medical support for treatment of complications especially in individuals with complicated medical conditions such as chronic leg ulcer, hip joint replacement, chronic respiratory conditions, etc

Welfare

TAHF SOCIAL WELFARE SERVICES

Provision of raw food items, clothing and educational support to the poor in the society with particular reference to sickle cellaffected families

Education

TAHF INFORMATION EDUCATIVE COMMUNICATION

TAHF through the use of electronic and prints media, inform, educate and create awareness on the management and prevention of sickle cell anaemia.

Research

TAHF RESEARCH PROGRAMME

TAHF supports specific research into therapeutic preparations that could aid the management of sickle cell anaemia.
